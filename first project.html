Ac Milan

Ac Milan in 1899

Associazione Calcio Milan (Italian pronunciation): commonly referred to as AC Milan or simply Milan, is a professional football club in Milan, Italy, founded in 1899.The club has spent its entire history, with the exception of the 1980–81 and 1982–83 seasons, in the top flight of Italian football, known as Serie A since 1929–30. Milan has won a joint record three Intercontinental Cups and one FIFA Club World Cup,[10] seven European Cup/Champions League titles (Italian record),[10] the UEFA Super Cup a joint record five times and the Cup Winners' Cup twice.[10] With 19 league titles, Milan is tied as the second most successful club in Serie A with local rivals Inter Milan (also with 19 league titles), behind Juventus (36 league titles).[11] They have also won the Coppa Italia five times, and the Supercoppa Italiana seven.[10]
